# ScreenLantern Roadmap

## Phase 0: MVP Foundation

- Establish app scaffold, auth, database schema, and local tooling
- Integrate TMDb catalog access
- Build search, browse, detail, library, and recommendation flows
- Launch household and saved group foundations

## Phase 1: Recommendation Quality

- Add stronger explanation metadata
- Improve candidate generation with trending and similarity inputs
- Incorporate provider preference weights more deeply
- Support richer group watch context and session history

## Phase 2: Automation and Intelligence

- Introduce AI-assisted recommendation explanations
- Add conversational discovery powered by the existing service layer
- Support mood, occasion, and time-budget prompts
- Generate "tonight plans" for a selected household group

## Phase 3: Integrations and Retention

- Add external provider deep links
- Support notification digests and reminders
- Add private sharing and recommendation handoffs inside a household
- Explore external sync partners for watch state and provider subscriptions

## Product Bets

- The biggest early differentiation is group recommendation quality
- The second biggest differentiator is low-friction context switching between solo and group modes
- AI should be layered on top of strong structured services, not used to replace them
